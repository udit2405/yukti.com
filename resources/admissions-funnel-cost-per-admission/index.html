The admissions funnel that lowers cost per admission

By Reckona AIUpdated 24 July 20268 min read

Every season, coaching institutes and schools spend more on ads and get less back — cost per admission climbs while leads slip between calls, sheets and WhatsApp. The fix isn't more spend; it's a funnel that stops leaking. Here's how it's built.

Why cost per admission climbs

Two reasons, usually. First, leads leak — a parent enquires, a counsellor is busy, and the follow-up never happens on schedule. Second, nobody measures cost per admission by source, so budget keeps flowing to channels that look busy but don't enrol.

The funnel that fixes it

StageWhat runs
AttractAdmission-season search & social ads, YouTube faculty content for trust
CaptureCourse landing pages + instant WhatsApp/CRM capture
NurtureCounsellor CRM automation: scheduled follow-ups, no lead goes cold
MeasureCost per admission by source, live — budget follows what enrols

Where automation earns its keep

Counsellors are good at conversations and bad at spreadsheets. Automating the follow-up cadence — reminders, parent updates, fee nudges — means every enquiry is worked on schedule while counsellors spend their time on the calls that convert. YouTube faculty content does the quiet trust-building before the counselling call even happens.

Measure by source or fly blind: once you know cost per admission per channel, cutting the losers and scaling the winners lowers your blended CPA without touching quality.
