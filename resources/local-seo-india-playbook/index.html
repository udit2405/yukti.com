Local SEO for Indian businesses: the 2026 playbook

By Reckona AIUpdated 24 July 20268 min read

For clinics, restaurants, showrooms and service businesses, the highest-intent search you can own is "near me". Here's the practical local SEO playbook we run in 2026 — and how AI answers are quietly changing local discovery.

Start with Google Business Profile

Your GBP is the single biggest lever in local SEO. Claim it, then treat it as a living asset: accurate categories, hours and services; real photos updated monthly; posts and offers; and Q&A seeded with the questions customers actually ask. An abandoned profile is the most common reason good local businesses lose the map pack to worse competitors.

The five pillars

  • Google Business Profile — categories, photos, posts, services, kept current.
  • Citations & NAP consistency — the same Name, Address, Phone across directories. Inconsistency confuses both Google and AI engines.
  • Reviews — a steady flow of genuine reviews, and replies to every one. Volume and recency both matter.
  • Local content — pages for each locality and service you serve, answering real local queries.
  • On-page & technical — LocalBusiness schema, fast mobile pages, embedded map.

What's changing: AI answers for local

Buyers increasingly ask ChatGPT or Gemini "where should I go for X near me". Those engines read your GBP, your reviews and third-party mentions. If your information is thin or inconsistent, AI describes you wrongly or omits you. Local SEO and GEO now reinforce each other — the same clean, consistent data feeds both.

Quick win: fix NAP consistency and reply to every review this month. Both move the map pack and both make you legible to AI answers — for near-zero cost.
